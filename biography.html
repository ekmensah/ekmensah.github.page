Biography

Emmanuel Kweku Mensah is an Demonstrator at the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT), Tarkwa Ghana in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering. He received his BSc degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Mines and Technology (UMaT). He has worked in this faculty position since September 2018. Prior to that, he served as a Teaching Assistant in the same department from September 2017 to August 2018.
